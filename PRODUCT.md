# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, research collaborators, and technical readers evaluating Saeed Arabha's materials R&D background. They arrive with limited time and need to understand technical depth, research direction, and industrial relevance quickly.

## Product Purpose

This website presents Saeed Arabha's academic and R&D profile through a portfolio, resume, research story, and technical case-study surfaces. Success means a visitor can understand the work, trust the expertise, and move naturally toward the resume, research progress, or direct contact.

## Brand Personality

Technical, precise, research-driven, and visually distinctive. The experience should feel like advanced materials work: layered, measured, engineered, and credible rather than generic SaaS, academic template, or decorative portfolio.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI-purple gradients, repetitive numbered section eyebrows, overused glass cards, plain academic CV styling, loud motivational copy, and fake-precise metrics without evidence. Avoid typography that shouts without clarifying the science.

## Design Principles

1. Show material thinking through structure, hierarchy, and motion.
2. Let process, evidence, and characterization carry the story.
3. Use bold typography only when it improves comprehension.
4. Preserve recruiter scanability while keeping the site visually memorable.
5. Keep claims specific, grounded, and technically credible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, responsive layouts, reduced-motion fallbacks, and avoid hiding essential content behind animation. Motion should support storytelling, not block comprehension.
